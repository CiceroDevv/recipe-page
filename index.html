An easy and quick dish, perfect for any mead. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionolly filed whidt your choise of cheese, vegetables, or meadts.

Preparation time

  • Total:

    Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation:

    5 minutes

  • Cooking:

    5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, coocked mearts, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:

    In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.cYou can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

  2. Heat the pan:

    Place a nom-stick fring pan over medium headt and add butter or oil.

  3. Cook the omeletter:

    Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the egges. tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly cost the surface.

  4. Add filling (optional):

    When the eggs begin to set ot the adges but are stillslighthly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chsen fillings over one half of the omelte.

  5. Fold and serve:

    As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.

  6. Enjoy:

    Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
